✏️ Drawing Tools

✨ Enhanced Line Tool: All elements are draggable with real-time measurements!
🎯 Bendable Lines: Double-click any line to add control points (orange dots) that let you bend the line into curves. Right-click control points to remove them.
🔄 Whole-Line Dragging: Drag anywhere on the line (not just endpoints) to move the entire line to a new position while maintaining its shape and curve.
Auto-MultiLine: Draw individual lines normally - after 2+ lines, they automatically combine into a MultiLineString in the GeoJSON output with total measurements.
ℹ️ Lines auto-combine into MultiLineString

📋 Generated GeoJSON

📥 Import GeoJSON

📥 Import Feature: Paste GeoJSON to automatically draw lines on the map.
🔄 Live Updates: When you modify imported lines, the GeoJSON automatically updates in real-time (minified format).
📋 Easy Copy: Use "Copy Edited" button or Shift+Ctrl+C to copy the minified edited GeoJSON.
